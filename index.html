Deskripsi

I developed an interactive multimedia for high school Physics, specifically focusing on the topic of fluids. This multimedia is designed to help students understand fluid concepts such as hydrostatic pressure, Pascal's law, and Archimedes' principle through visualization, animations, and interactive exercises.

The main features of this multimedia include: Educational animations to visually explain physics concepts, Interactive simulations that allow students to conduct virtual experiments, Interactive quizzes with instant feedback, A user-friendly interface accessible on both desktop and mobile devices..

Deskripsi

As part of the development of Interactive Multimedia (MMI) for high school Physics on the topic of fluids, I also prepared supporting materials that can be used by teachers and students as a guide to using this multimedia effectively, both in the classroom and independently
